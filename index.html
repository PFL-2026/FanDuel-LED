★ New Activation Idea

A co-branded LED wristband activation: attendees receive a FanDuel LED wristband on entry, turning the arena into a synchronised sea of FanDuel blue for TV cameras and social capture.

  • Universal distribution — co-branded FanDuel LED wristbands for all attendees
  • 3 × in-event moments — randomly selected wristband “lights up” three times throughout the night, locating a winning fan in-crowd
  • Winners receive co-branded PFL & FanDuel merch, presented on the night
  • TV production awareness — broadcast cut-ins to the lit-up fan moments, building three storylines through the card
  • Consistent visual branding across the arena — synced colour, logo lockup, fan-led activation
